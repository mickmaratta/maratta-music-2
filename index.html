About Us

At Maratta Music we've been helping others for the last 10+ years not only learn how to play music but how to love it. We accept all ages and levels. With some of the best teachers and musicians in the GTA we are able to tailor a curriculum to fit your specific goals.

Whether we're driving, watching TV, or putting on a old vinyl record, music impacts our life everyday. Although music surrounds us all the time, many of us spend very little time getting to understand and appreciate the art.
